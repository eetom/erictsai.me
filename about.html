ABOUT ME

Eric Tsai

I’ve been designing and coding websites and mobile apps over the past 5 years. Some of my previous work includes collaborations with startups and agencies on design projects that cover typography, vector graphics, user experience, branding, and interface design.

When I’m not busy pushing pixels, I am constantly getting involved with startups. In the past I’ve co-founded a design agency in Vancouver and was an integral part of a startup that graduated from the FounderFuel accelerator to a full product.

I’m always learning and taking on new challenges in design and entrepreneurship, and look forward to creating new design solutions and awesome experiences for users.
