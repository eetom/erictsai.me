ABOUT ME

Eric Tsai

– Designer

I’ve been concocting websites and mobile apps over the past 4 years. Some of my previous work includes collaborations with startups and agencies on design projects that cover typography, vector graphics, user experience, branding, and interface design.

When I’m not busy pushing pixels, I am constantly getting involved with startups. In the past I’ve co-founded a design agency in Vancouver and was integral part of a startup that graduated from an accelerator program to a full product.

I’m always learning and taking on new challenges in design and entrepreneurship, and look forward to creating new design solutions and awesome experiences for users.

SKILLS

What kind of design do I do?



Graphic Design

Graphic design is my bread and butter. My degree had a focus on graphic design and I began my career with branding and marketing graphics. I have a keen eye for color and fonts and always keeping up to date with the latest in graphic design.

UI / Layout Design

Ever since I graduated I’ve been intrigued by design on digital mediums, primarily web browsers and mobile apps. Applying and learning how to creating fun and usable interfaces has been a key goal as a designer.

User Experience

Focusing on the affordances and needs of the user leads to more enjoyable products and the difference between good design and great design. This is the foundation of all my design through user test studies to rapid prototyping.
